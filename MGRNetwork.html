About MGR Network (My Genre Radio FM)

My Genre Radio FM was created to bring back real music that was lost in this new music era.

Each genre of today's music either sounds the same or has repeated loops and tunes.

Each song from today's music is more about anything but music.

My Genre Radio FM can bring you back to the truly authentic reason for songs bringing people together and relaxing their souls.

My Genre Radio FM brings music from Old-School Hip-Hop, R&B, Rock, Blues, Classical, Country, the 60s, the 70s, the 80s, and the 90s, Electronic, Jazz, Reggae, Talk Radio and More.

This is a music network that you can listen to 24/7. You will hear DJs and hosts that cater to the audience's listening experience.

My Genre Radio Network delivers the new independent artists that play the genres that are lost and showcase them to apply with their talent and their skills in their local area.

It's Your Business Radio Show

“It’s Your Business” radio show, founded in 2011, co-hosted by Dr. Stan Fine PhD and Jim Jump, MBA, which began as a weekly one hour long show broadcasting on a small St. Louis neighborhood network, has skyrocketed at meteoric speed with over two million listeners from coast to coast.

Interviewing experts from the field of marketing, finance, PR, cyber security and other small business support industries, the show provides support and information to entrepreneurs and small businesses seeking help to take their business to the next level.
Listen to It's Your Business Radio show weekdays @ 9 AM CST, 10 AM EST & 7 AM PST and 1 PM CST, 2 PM EST & 11 AM PST on My Genre Radio FM.
