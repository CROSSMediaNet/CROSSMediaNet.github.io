Technically Speaking (Coming Fall 2026)

Technically Speaking is a dark comedy that dives into the chaotic life of Elliot, a socially awkward IT technician trapped in the monotony of PC support at a financial and tax services firm. Between fixing printers for clueless coworkers, navigating the mixed signals of Megan—the office flirt he's secretly in love with—and dodging the wrath of his condescending boss, Elliot’s work life is a comedy of errors.

As frustrations mount and his mundane existence spirals into absurdity, Elliot finally snaps. With a wickedly dark sense of humor and an arsenal of tech-savvy tricks, he turns the tables on those who’ve wronged him, wreaking havoc on his office and personal life in hilariously unpredictable ways.

Technically Speaking is a riotous exploration of the mundane turned insane—a modern workplace comedy with a dark, twisted edge.

Lyonel: A Warring Wretch

A gripping tale of redemption, faith, and vengeance. Once a ruthless assassin, Lyonel’s life took a drastic turn when a preacher introduced him to the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Leaving behind a life of bloodshed, Lyonel sought peace as a farmer’s assistant, embracing his faith, marrying the love of his life, and starting a family.

But peace is fleeting in a land that has turned its back on God. When an assassin’s cult—led by the vengeful Vanessa—destroys everything Lyonel holds dear, he is forced to confront his dark past. Fueled by grief and righteous fury, Lyonel sets out on a relentless quest for justice, reclaiming the skills he swore he’d left behind.

In a harrowing battle against evil, Lyonel must confront not only his enemies but also his own inner turmoil, seeking redemption for his actions in a world that demands his soul. Will his faith guide him, or will vengeance consume him?

Lyonel: A Warring Wretch is an intense journey of sacrifice, wrath, and the struggle to hold onto faith when everything is lost.
